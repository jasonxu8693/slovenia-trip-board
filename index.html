Planning watchouts

💶

Euro + budget

Slovenia uses the euro. Set a per-person budget before comparing villa, apartment cluster, cars and food tiers.

🚗

Vignette + ZTL

Hire cars need a motorway vignette — confirm it's included. Ljubljana old town is largely car-free; park on the edge.

🧥

Caves are cold

Postojna is ~10°C year-round. Bring a layer even on a warm September day. Tours are timed — pre-book.

🌊

Vintgar crowds

Vintgar is a timed, one-way boardwalk. Book an early slot and treat it as a planned entry, not a casual stop.
