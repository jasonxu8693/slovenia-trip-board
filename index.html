Essentials 🇸🇮

The on-the-ground cheat sheet — save this to your phone before Trieste.

Schengen — no border stop ✅ € euro both sides Vignette for the motorway
💶

Money

Italy and Slovenia both use the euro — one currency for the whole trip.

  • Cards work almost everywhere; carry a little cash for huts, parking machines and kiosks.
  • Slovenia is noticeably cheaper than the UK for food and beer.
  • Log shared spend as you go so the last night isn't a spreadsheet.
📱

Data & apps

Most UK plans roam in the EU — check your cap, or grab a cheap eSIM.

  • Google Maps works great here — still download offline maps for the Julian Alps.
  • Book Vintgar and the Postojna / Škocjan tours online before the day.
🚘

Driving

Cars come from Trieste and cross the border — no checkpoint, but get it right:

  • Confirm cross-border use is on the rental agreement.
  • Buy the Slovenian e-vignette before touching the motorway.
  • Italian autostrada tolls; Ljubljana old town is car-free — park on the edge.

🆘 If something goes wrong

🚓

Emergency numbers

  • 112 — EU-wide emergency (medical, fire, mountain rescue)
  • 113 — Police (Slovenia)
  • 1987 — AMZS roadside assistance (Slovenia)
  • Save the rental's breakdown number offline too.
🛂

Entry & admin

  • Schengen — one passport check at Trieste, then no border stop into Slovenia.
  • UK passports: issued under 10 years ago and valid 3+ months past the return date.
  • Bring your GHIC/EHIC + travel insurance; allow buffer for entry kiosks at Trieste.

🧳 Good to know

🧥

Mid-Sep weather

Roughly 10–22°C — warm lake afternoons, cool alpine evenings, a real chance of a rain day. Layers + a shell. The caves are ~10°C year-round, so bring a jumper even if it's hot outside.

🔌

Plugs & power

Type C / F sockets, 230V — standard Europe. One UK→EU adapter each.

🍰

Eat & drink

  • Kremšnita at Lake Bled — the non-negotiable ritual.
  • Štruklji, big alpine portions; Laško vs Union is the local beer debate.
  • Tipping: round up ~10% for good service. Tap water is excellent.

🗣️ A few phrases

🙂

The basics

  • Dober dan — DOH-ber dahn · hello / good day
  • Hvala — HVAH-la · thank you
  • Prosim — PRO-seem · please / you're welcome
  • Ja / Ne — yes / no
🍻

Out & about

  • Koliko stane? — KO-lee-ko STAH-neh · how much?
  • Kje je stranišče? — kyeh yeh stra-NEESH-cheh · where's the toilet?
  • Na zdravje! — na ZDRAV-yeh · cheers!
  • Eno pivo, prosim — one beer, please
